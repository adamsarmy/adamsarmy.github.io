2020 Adams Army Golf Tournament

We are so excited to be hosting our first annual golf tournament on  Saturday May 30th, 2020 at Edmonton Garrison Memorial Golf & Curling Club. The registration for a golfer is $125 and this will include a buffet dinner. If anyone would like to have a guest join for just the dinner portion there is a cost of $35.

Please fill out the form below and E-transfer your registration fees to golf@adamsarmy.ca. When filling out the form please include a breakdown of payment info for your etransfer in the comments section.;


Example

Golfer 1 - Happy Gilmore $125

Golfer 2 - Shooter Mcgavin $125

Dinner Guest - Chubbs Peterson $35

Donation to fund - $30

Total Payment - $315



Thank you for your continued support and can't wait to see everyone at the course.

Thanks you for registering!
